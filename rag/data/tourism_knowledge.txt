Amer Fort:
Location: Jaipur
Timings: 8:00 AM – 5:30 PM
Best Time: Morning or evening
Description: A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its grand architecture and history.
Tips: Visit early to avoid crowd and heat.

City Palace Jaipur:
Location: Jaipur
Timings: 9:30 AM – 5:00 PM
Best Time: Morning
Description: Royal residence with museums and courtyards.
Tips: Buy composite ticket for full access.

Hawa Mahal:
Location: Jaipur
Timings: 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM
Best Time: Early morning
Description: Iconic palace with 953 windows for airflow.
Tips: Best viewed from outside for photos.

Govind Devji Temple:
Location: Jaipur
Timings: 4:30 AM – 9:00 PM (multiple aarti slots)
Best Time: During aarti
Description: Famous Krishna temple with strong spiritual significance.
Tips: Visit during aarti for best experience.

Jal Mahal:
Location: Jaipur
Timings: View anytime (outside only)
Best Time: Evening
Description: Palace in the middle of Man Sagar Lake.
Tips: Great for sunset photography.

Nahargarh Fort:
Location: Jaipur
Timings: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
Best Time: Sunset
Description: Hilltop fort with panoramic city view.
Tips: Ideal for evening visit.
